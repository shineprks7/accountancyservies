Know the Health of Your Business (For Small Services Business)

Stop measuring your business by how busy you are and start measuring it by how free you are. This 15-question assessment identifies the specific bottlenecks in your SME, moving you from a "founder-dependent" hustle to a scalable, prosperous asset.

About This Tool

Is your business a "Freedom Machine" or just a high-stress job?

Most service-based SME owners are so busy in the business that they rarely get the chance to work on the business. The SME Business Prosperity Assessment was designed to give you a high-level, honest look at the health of your company beyond just the balance sheet.

While traditional accounting tools tell you what happened in the past, this tool measures your future readiness. By evaluating four critical pillars—Financial Vitality, Operational Efficiency, Market Reputation, and Personal Sanity—this quiz identifies exactly where your "growth friction" is located.

Whether you are a solo consultant, a creative agency, or a local service provider, this assessment provides a 2026-ready roadmap to move from "Hustle" to "Prosperity."

Why Use This Tool?

In the service sector, your time is your most precious inventory. If you are stuck in a cycle of "lumpy" cash flow or feel like the business would collapse if you took a week off, you aren't yet prosperous—you are just busy.

Use this tool to:

  • Identify Bottlenecks: Pinpoint exactly which area of your business is holding back your growth.
  • Benchmark Your Freedom: Measure how dependent the business is on your personal manual labor.
  • Audit Your Finances: See if you are truly profitable or just "subsidizing" the business with unpaid owner hours.
  • Prioritize Your 2026 Goals: Stop guessing what to fix next and start following a data-driven action plan.

How to Use This Tool

This assessment is designed to be completed in less than 5 minutes. To get the most accurate results, follow these simple steps:

  • Be Brutally Honest: This tool is for your eyes only. Answer based on the reality of your business today, not where you hope it will be next year.
  • Choose the "Best Fit": For each of the 15 questions, select any one option from the given options that most closely describes your current situation.
  • Review Your "Pillar" Scores: After finishing, look at which of the four categories scored the lowest. This is your "Priority One" zone for the next 90 days.

Who Is This For?

  • Service-Based SMEs: Agencies, consultants, tradespeople, and professional service firms.
  • Founders & Owners: Who feel "stuck" in the day-to-day operations.
  • Growing Teams: Who need to transition from "founder-led" to "system-led" growth.
